General Information

What is NYC Singles Dance?

  • Themed Music Nights – From Salsa and Top 40 hits to 80s/90s classics, Electronic, and Jazz, each event showcases a distinct musical theme, appealing to diverse musical tastes.

Venue and Safety Protocols

What are our venue requirements?

Our venue standards ensure a high-quality, safe, and convenient experience:

  • Capacity – Minimum 150-200 guests.
  • Dance Floor – At least 800 sq. ft. of designated dance space.
  • Sound System – Professional-grade with at least 4000W output.
  • Accessibility – Located within a 10-minute walk of public transit options.
  • Amenities – Separate coat check, at least two bars, and a designated quiet area.

What security measures are in place?

We prioritize attendee safety with the following protocols:

  • Entry Screening
    • ID checks using UV light verification.
    • Metal detector wanding.
    • Bag size restrictions, limited to 8"x10".
  • On-Site Security
    • 1 security officer per 50 attendees, with a minimum of two female officers.
    • All personnel are trained in de-escalation techniques.
  • Emergency Protocols
    • Direct communication line to local law enforcement.
    • On-call medical professionals at every event.
    • Clearly marked emergency exits with illuminated signage.
